Q4 2024 Financial Report

Period: October - December 2024
Date: January 15, 2025
Prepared by: Finance Team

Executive Summary

Q4 2024 delivered strong results across all key financial metrics. Revenue grew 12.5% quarter-over-quarter to $2.4M, driven primarily by enterprise software sales and new customer acquisition in the mid-market segment.

Key highlights for the quarter:

  • Revenue: $2.4M (+12.5% vs Q3, +18.7% YoY)
  • Net Profit: $620K with 25.8% margin
  • Customer Growth: 170 new customers (+15.7%)
  • Retention Rate: 94% (up from 91% in Q3)

Operating expenses increased 3.2% due to planned investments in sales and engineering headcount. These investments are expected to drive continued growth in 2025.

2025 Outlook

Based on current pipeline and market conditions, we project continued growth in 2025:

  • Q1 2025 Revenue: $2.5-2.7M (projected 8-12% growth)
  • Full Year 2025: $11-12M total revenue target
  • Headcount: Plan to add 15 new positions across Sales and Engineering

Key initiatives for 2025 include expansion into European markets and launch of the Enterprise Plus tier.
